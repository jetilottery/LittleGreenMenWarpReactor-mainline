Little Green Men Warp Reactor

Explore interstellar riches in Little Green Men Warp Reactor!
Match 3 or more identical, adjacent planets to create clusters. The clusters disappear and the score for each planet increases as new planets fall in from above. Win a prize when you collect enough identical planets. Trigger the Warp Bonus when you collect 4 UFOs – you might even win a prize instantly! Collect 4 asteroids and trigger the Whirl Bonus: the planets may shift position on the grid, where they may be randomly collected to create new clusters and even better chances at winning a prize!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Little Green Men Warp Reactor is to match 3 or more identical, adjacent planets.

When 3 or more identical planets are adjacent in the grid, they create clusters. The clusters are removed and the score for each planet increases accordingly. Collect the required number of any planet to win a prize. Multiple prizes are available for each planet.

Collect 4 UFOs to trigger the Warp Bonus and collect additional planets. Collect a Star symbol to win a prize instantly.

Collect 4 asteroids to trigger the Whirl Bonus. Planets may change position on the grid, where they may be randomly collected or form new clusters.

A bonus game is triggered only when no clusters remain on the grid.

Play ends when no clusters remain on the grid and all the bonus games are completed. A message appears indicating the game's outcome.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.
